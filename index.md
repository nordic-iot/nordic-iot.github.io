**HI2OT — the Nordic University Hub on Industrial Internet of Things** was a research-and-education collaboration of five leading Nordic universities. Its overall aim was to promote Nordic collaboration in Industrial IoT, increasing the capacity of the participating organizations by creating a critical mass for a world-leading Nordic research environment in Industrial IoT.

[Read more about HI2OT](/about/)
